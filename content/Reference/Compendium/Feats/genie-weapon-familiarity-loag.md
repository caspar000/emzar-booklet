# Genie Weapon Familiarity  *Feat 1*  
[ifrit](ifrit-b2.md "Ifrit Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  [oread](oread-b2.md "Oread Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  [suli](suli-b2.md "Suli Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  [sylph](sylph-b2.md "Sylph Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  [undine](undine-b2.md "Undine Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  


You've trained with weapons used by your genie ancestors. You are trained with [falchions](falchion.md), [ranseurs](ranseur.md), [scimitars](scimitar.md), and [tridents](trident.md). In addition, you gain access to all uncommon geniekin weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial geniekin weapons are simple weapons and advanced geniekin weapons are martial weapons.
