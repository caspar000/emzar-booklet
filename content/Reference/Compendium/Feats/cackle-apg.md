# Cackle  *Feat 1*  
[witch](Reference/Rules/Traits/witch-apg.md "Witch Class Trait")  


You can extend one of your spells with a quick burst of laughter. You learn the [cackle](Reference/Compendium/Spells/cackle-apg.md) hex. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

*Source: Advanced Player's Guide p. 100*
