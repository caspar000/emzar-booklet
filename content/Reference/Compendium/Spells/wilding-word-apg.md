# Wilding Word *Cantrip 1*

- **Patron** Witch
- **Cast** [>](chapter-9-playing-the-game.md#Actions "Single Action") 
- **Range**: 30 foot
- **Targets**: 1 animal, fungus, or plant
- **Saving Throw**: Will
- **Duration**: sustained up to 1 minute
