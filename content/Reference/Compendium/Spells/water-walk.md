# Water Walk *Spell 2*   
[transmutation](transmutation.md "Transmutation School Trait")  

- **Traditions** [arcane](arcane.md "Arcane Tradition Trait"), [divine](divine.md "Divine Tradition Trait"), [primal](primal.md "Primal Tradition Trait")
- **Cast** [>>](chapter-9-playing-the-game.md#Actions "Two-Action") 
- **Range**: touch
- **Targets**: 1 creature
- **Duration**: 10 minutes

The target can walk on the surface of water and other liquids without falling through. It can go underwater if it wishes, but in that case it must [Swim](swim.md) normally. This spell doesn't grant the ability to breathe underwater.

**Heightened (4th)** The spell's range increases to 30 feet, the duration increases to 1 hour, and you can target up to 10 creatures.

**Spell Lists**: Elemental
